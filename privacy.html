Privacy Policy

Effective June 14, 2026

Resin (“the app”) is a macOS application by Danilo Sierra (“we,” “us”). The short version: Resin does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data we collect

None. Resin has no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. We receive no information about you or how you use the app.

How the app works

  • Websites you capture. When you enter a URL, Resin loads that page in a private, offscreen web view on your Mac to take a screenshot or recording. That connection is directly between your Mac and the website you chose — exactly like opening it in a browser. We do not see, log, or store the URLs you enter or the pages you capture.
  • Your files. Screenshots and videos you export are saved only to the location you pick on your Mac. Nothing is ever uploaded.
  • Screen Recording permission. If you enable it, Resin uses it solely to record its own offscreen web view at a higher frame rate. It never captures your desktop, other windows, or other applications.

Third parties

Resin shares no data with anyone, because it collects none.

Children

Resin collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

We may update this policy; updates will appear on this page with a new effective date.
